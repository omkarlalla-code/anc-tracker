# WHO 2016 ANC Visit Schedule Reference

## 4-Visit Focused Model

| Visit | Timing | Purpose |
|-------|--------|---------|
| 1 | Before 12 weeks | Confirmation and baseline screening |
| 2 | 20-24 weeks | PIH and anemia screening |
| 3 | 28-32 weeks | Multiple pregnancy exclusion |
| 4 | 36-40 weeks | Birth preparedness |

## Reminder Schedule
- 7 days before visit
- 2 days before visit
- Missed visit alert (after scheduled date)

## Reference
WHO recommendations on antenatal care for a positive pregnancy experience (2016)
